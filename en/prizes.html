Prizes

One winning team per track — gift cards, Hexaly merch, travel support, and a stage at CSMIO 2026.

Each track (Undergraduate and Open) has one winning team, determined by the highest score under the lead-time scoring system.

🎁

Amazon Gift Cards

Undergraduate Track: $100 USD per person ($400 USD per team, including the guide).

Open Track: $200 USD per person ($600 USD per team).

Total prize pool: $1,000 USD in gift cards.

👕

Hexaly Merch

Every member of each winning team receives 1 Hexaly hoodie + 1 Hexaly t-shirt (up to 3 members per team, plus the guide for the Undergraduate track).

✈️

Travel Support to CSMIO 2026

One member from each winning team will be sponsored to attend CSMIO 2026 in Monterrey, Mexico. Travel, accommodation, and conference registration are covered.

🎤

Presentation at CSMIO 2026

Winning teams are invited to present their approaches at a dedicated Challenge session during the XIV CSMIO. Share your methods with the Latin American OR community.

For All Participants

Free Academic Licenses

Hexaly provides free academic licenses to all students and academics. You don't need to use Hexaly to compete, but it's available if you want to explore it.

Permanent Benchmark

All final solutions (but not code) will be published as part of a permanent benchmark library for the CLRP. Your work contributes to the research community regardless of your final ranking.
