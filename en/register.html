§ Team registration

Captain registers.
Teammates confirm.

As captain, enter your team's info and your teammates' emails. Each teammate gets one email with a magic link to set their password and confirm. Once everyone confirms, the team is locked in.

Any team member can submit solutions — lead-time scoring rewards speed, so there's no bottleneck through the captain.
