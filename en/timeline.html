Timeline

From announcement to award ceremony — key dates for the Challenge.

The competition window is approximately 3 weeks in July 2026. Teams will have had access to the instance generator for weeks before the competition opens, so the official window is an execution sprint on the real instances — not a development period from scratch.

April 22–24, 2026
EPIO & Challenge Announcement
The Challenge is officially announced at the closing of the V EPIO (SMIO Virtual Spring School). Call for participation, rules, and problem description published. Team registration opens.
May – June 2026
Preparation Period
Teams form and register. Continued dissemination through SMIO and Hexaly channels. Development of the instance generator, solution verifier, and web platform.
June 2026
Instance Generator Released
Open-source Python code released so teams can create test instances and develop their methods. A sample instance with a feasible solution and the verifier code are also published. Official competition instances are not published at this stage.
June – July 2026
Final Preparation
Generation of official instances (internal). Hexaly runs its solver to produce the baseline solutions. Platform testing and final checks.
Mid-July 2026
Competition Launch
Official instances and Hexaly baseline solutions published. Solution submission opens. The leaderboard goes live.
Mid-July – Early August 2026
Competition Window (~3 weeks)
Teams submit solutions through the platform. The leaderboard tracks the best participant solutions in real time.
Early August 2026
Competition Close
No more submissions accepted. Final verification of all solutions begins.
August 2026
Results & Coordination
Results analysis and winner determination. Contact with winning teams to coordinate presentations and travel logistics for CSMIO.
September 2026
Conference Preparation
Preparation of the Challenge session for the CSMIO program. Results dissemination materials.
October 7–9, 2026
CSMIO 2026 in Monterrey
Challenge results presentation session. Hexaly workshop. Award ceremony. Winners present their approaches to the community.
Post-Conference
Permanent Benchmark
Publication of all results, instances, and solutions as a permanent benchmark for the research community.

Note on Dates

Exact competition launch and close dates will be confirmed once the instance generator is finalized. The dates above are tentative and subject to minor adjustments. Follow SMIO's channels for official updates.
